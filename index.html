Frequently Asked Questions

Atlanta Transportation Questions

How do I reserve BlackShield Transportation?

Complete the online reservation form, calculate your estimated rate, choose an available payment option, and continue to secure Stripe Checkout. BlackShield will review the request and confirm vehicle availability.

Can I pay a deposit instead of the full amount?

Eligible reservations may offer a 25% deposit, a 50% deposit, or full payment. Trips scheduled within 24 hours require full payment.

Do you provide transportation from ATL Airport?

Yes. BlackShield provides private sedan and SUV airport transportation between Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport and destinations throughout Metro Atlanta.

Do you offer hourly chauffeur service?

Yes. Sedan, SUV, Sprinter, limousine, and group vehicle reservations may be booked at hourly rates with applicable minimum-hour requirements.

Does online payment guarantee my vehicle?

No. Payment submits and secures the reservation request, but the reservation remains pending until BlackShield confirms the trip and vehicle availability.

What areas does BlackShield serve?

Service is available throughout Atlanta, Buckhead, Midtown, Downtown, Duluth, Alpharetta, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Lawrenceville, and many surrounding Metro Atlanta communities. Custom Georgia transportation is available by request.
